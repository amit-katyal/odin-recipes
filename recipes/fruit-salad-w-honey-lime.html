Fruit salad with honey lime dressing

Burst of Flavor: Try This Zesty Honey Lime Fruit Salad Recipe

A colorful and refreshing salad made with mixed fruit such as strawberries, kiwi, and pineapple, dressed with a honey-lime dressing.

A bowl of summer fruits drizzled with a sweet and tangy honey lime dressing. Refreshing, delicious and full of great fruity flavors! An easy side for any meals, potlucks and parties

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed fresh fruit (such as strawberries, kiwi, pineapple, and mango), diced
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon fresh mint leaves, chopped
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced mixed fruit.
  2. In a separate small bowl, whisk together the honey, fresh lime juice, chopped mint leaves, and salt until well combined.
  3. Pour the honey lime dressing over the mixed fruit and gently toss to coat.
  4. Serve the fruit salad immediately or cover and refrigerate until ready to serve.

Enjoy your refreshing and delicious fruit salad with honey lime dressing!
