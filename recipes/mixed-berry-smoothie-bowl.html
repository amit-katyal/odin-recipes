Mixed Berry smoothie bowl

The Ultimate Mixed Berry Smoothie Bowl Recipe: Perfect for a Summer Morning

A delicious and healthy breakfast or snack option made with mixed berries, greek yogurt, and granola.

Enjoy for breakfast or as a healthy afternoon snack! Loaded with antioxidants and sweet, smooth flavor!

Ingredients:

  • 1 frozen banana, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed frozen berries (such as strawberries, blueberries, and raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup almond milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • Toppings (such as granola, sliced fruit, shredded coconut, chia seeds)

Instructions:

  1. Add the frozen banana, mixed frozen berries, Greek yogurt, almond milk, and honey (if using) to a blender.
  2. Blend the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy, stopping to scrape down the sides of the blender as needed.
  3. Pour the mixed berry smoothie into a bowl.
  4. Top the smoothie with your desired toppings, such as granola, sliced fruit, shredded coconut, and chia seeds.
  5. Serve the mixed berry smoothie bowl immediately and enjoy!

This mixed berry smoothie bowl is a delicious and healthy breakfast or snack option that is perfect for a hot summer day.
