Mango Yogurt Recipe: A Refreshing Frozen Treat

Looking for a refreshing and healthy dessert to cool you down on a hot summer day? Look no further than our Frozen Mango Yogurt!

This delicious treat combines creamy Greek yogurt with sweet and juicy frozen mango chunks for a tropical twist on traditional frozen yogurt.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups frozen mango chunks
  • 1 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, add the frozen mango chunks, Greek yogurt, honey, milk, and vanilla extract. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour the mixture into a freezer-safe container and freeze for at least 2 hours or until firm.
  3. Once the mixture is frozen, remove it from the freezer and let it sit at room temperature for a few minutes to soften.
  4. Scoop the frozen yogurt into bowls or cones and serve immediately.
  5. Optional: Garnish with fresh mango chunks or a sprinkle of toasted coconut flakes for an extra tropical touch.

Enjoy your delicious and healthy Mango Tango Yogurt!
